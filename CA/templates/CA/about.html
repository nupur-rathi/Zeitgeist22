About Us


Zeitgeist, one of the biggest annual cultural fests of North India, where you can experience the perfect blend of art, talent, and diversity under one roof. It has a total footfall of about 12,000 and a reach of more than 250 colleges. Zeitgeist is an opportunity to gain valuable experience, build a strong network with people with zeal and enthusiasm, and witness a gala. It provides a platform for the young dabsters of our nation to set the stage on fire. Many celebrated personalities like Diljit Dosanjh, Vishal-Shekhar, Mohit Chauhan, Alfaz, Zakir Khan, and DJ Olly Esse have added to the glory of the fest. So to bid adieu to the curse of covid 19, we bring to you Zeitgeist 2k22.





Why Become Campus Ambassador?


Leadership Skills:

To become a leader in the world of followers. Get a glimpse of the corporate world and learn about the working of organisations.

Communication Expertise :

An opportunity to improve your communication and managerial skills.

Certificate of Appreciation:

Get a certificate from Zeitgeist, IIT Ropar after completion of tenure.

Auxiliary Benefits:

Grab official Zeitgeist 2k22 merchandise and receive goodies from our sponsors.

Building your confidence:

To gain skills like team management, event organisation, public speaking, social media, content writing and promotion, and various other technical skills.

Meeting New People:

To get connected with various like-minded students across various campuses.

Get Industry Certified:

Add Campus Ambassador experience in your resume and get ahead of others.


Incentives



  • CAs who bring more than 50 participants will get goodies and t-shirts of Zeitgeist 2k22 for free.
  • CAs will get cash prizes based on the number of participants referred by them.
  • All CAs will get certificate of participation.



Responsibilities



  • Be a leader :
    • Lead the contingent from your college. Get students of your college acquainted with Zeitgeist.
  • Bring maximum participation :
    • Ensure maximum registration and participation from your college.
  • Publicise Zeitgeist :
    • Encourage others to like and follow Zeitgeist's Instagram and Facebook pages. Use 'Invite All' feature on Facebook. Share pages, events' posters and posts on Facebook and Whatsapp. Paste Zeitgeist's posters at active and lively sites of your college.



Frequently Asked Questions



  • What is Campus Ambassador?
    • Campus Ambassadors are active leaders in their college who act as representatives of Zeitgeist.
  • How to apply for Campus Ambassador?
    • By clicking on 'Register' button at the top of this page.
  • What is the eligibility to become a Campus Ambassador?
    • One must own valid ID card issued in his/her name from the college whose Campus Ambassador he/she wants to be.
  • Can there be more than one Campus Ambassador from the same college?
    • If student participation is relatively high from a college, then more than one Campus Ambassadors may be appointed.
